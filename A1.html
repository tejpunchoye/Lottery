Prediction strategies
1
Recency-weighted frequency — newer draws contribute more weight.
2
Row activity boost — numbers from most active row get +30% weight.
3
Normalised probability sampling — weighted random without replacement.
4
Validation filters — 2–4 odd, 2–4 low (1–25), sum 100–200, no consecutive numbers.
5
Generate-and-filter loop — up to 5k candidates.
Data sources
All draws & arrangement are loaded from local files using the file picker. Once loaded, they are stored in memory. You can also add more draws manually.
